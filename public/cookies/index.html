Cookie notice

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help a website work and remember your preferences. This notice explains how DBBet uses them.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary — required for the site to work, keep you logged in and keep your account secure. These can't be switched off.
  • Preferences — remember choices such as language and display settings.
  • Analytics — help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. These are anonymised where possible.
  • Marketing — used to measure campaigns and, where you agree, show more relevant offers. These are only set with your consent.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings — including deleting existing cookies and blocking new ones. Blocking some cookies may affect how the site works.

Third parties

Some cookies are set by trusted third parties, such as analytics and payment providers. Their use of data is covered by their own policies. See our privacy policy for more on how we handle data.

Changes

We may update this notice as our use of cookies changes. The version on this page is the one that applies.
