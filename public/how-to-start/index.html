Add DBBet to your iPhone

There's no App Store download — instead you add DBBet to your home screen, which gives you the same full-screen, app-like experience.

  1. Open dbbetke.com in the Safari browser (this won't work in Chrome).
  2. Tap the Share icon in the toolbar (the square with an arrow).
  3. Scroll down the menu and choose "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Keep the name as DBBet and tap Add — the icon lands on your home screen.
  5. Open it like any app, log in, deposit with M-Pesa and play.

If the app won't install or open

  • Allow installs from your browser: Settings → Apps → Special access → Install unknown apps.
  • Check you have enough free storage (about 100 MB) and a stable connection.
  • Delete the old file, download the latest APK again, and reinstall.
  • Restart your phone, then open the app once more.
  • Still stuck? Just use the mobile site in your browser — it works with nothing to install.

Handy mobile tips

App FAQ

Questions about the DBBet app

How do I install the APK on Android?
Tap Download APK, open the downloaded file, allow installs from your browser if asked, then tap Install and Open.
Can I use it on an iPhone?
Yes. Open the site in Safari, tap Share, then "Add to Home Screen" for an app-like icon — no App Store download needed.
Why does Android ask about "unknown sources"?
Because the app installs from the web instead of the Play Store. It's a normal one-time permission — just allow installs from your browser.
What if the app doesn't open?
Free up space, re-download the latest APK and reinstall, or restart your phone. You can always fall back to the mobile site.
How much storage do I need?
Around 100 MB of free space for the Android app.
Can I play without installing anything?
Yes — you can bet and play straight from your mobile browser. The app just makes it quicker to open and reach your account.
